## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the installer">
    Visit [vencord.dev/download](https://vencord.dev/download) and download the installer for your platform (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the installer">
    Run the installer and follow the prompts. It will automatically detect your Discord installation and inject Vencord.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Discord">
    Start Discord. You'll see the Vencord settings appear in your Discord settings under the **Vencord** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable plugins">
    Open **Settings → Plugins** and enable any of the 100+ built-in plugins you want to use. Changes take effect immediately or after a restart depending on the plugin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Vencord?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="100+ built-in plugins" icon="plug">
    From message timestamps to Spotify controls, Vencord ships with a huge library of community-built plugins ready to enable with one click.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Privacy first" icon="shield">
    Vencord blocks Discord analytics and crash reporting out of the box. No telemetry, no tracking beyond what Discord itself does.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom themes" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Import any CSS theme including BetterDiscord-compatible themes, or write your own CSS with the built-in editor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Browser support" icon="globe">
    Run Vencord in Chrome or Firefox via the browser extension, or as a UserScript — no desktop app required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Settings sync" icon="cloud">
    Optionally sync your plugin configuration and settings across multiple devices and Discord clients.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Actively maintained" icon="wrench">
    Broken plugins are typically fixed within 12 hours. Vencord tracks Discord's updates and keeps plugins working.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
